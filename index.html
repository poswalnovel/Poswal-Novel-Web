The Story Behind the Page

We Are PoswalNovel

PoswalNovel started in 2026 out of a simple love for books — the kind that keep you up past midnight and leave you staring at the ceiling long after the last page. We're a community-first literary brand dedicated to helping readers across India, Pakistan and the world discover their next favourite novel.

We cover Urdu, Hindi and English literature with the same warmth: honest reviews, heartfelt recommendations, and a genuine passion for storytelling. No paid promotions. No biased opinions. Just books, words, and the readers who love them.

  • 📖Honest, spoiler-free reviews — always
  • 🌐Books across Urdu, Hindi & English
  • 🌏Readers from India, Pakistan & worldwide
  • 💛Community-first — no paid promotions, ever
